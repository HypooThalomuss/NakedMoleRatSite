Introduction

  1. What is the website about?
    • The website will focus on the social structure of naked mole rats. More specifically, it will be about the colony structure of the naked mole rat and how that helps it to survive in various aspects such as predator defense, mating, etc.
  2. What is its purpose (can be more than one)?
    • The research for this site will be based off of the research I did for a previous class, this website will just allow me to put this research into a form more digestable than a research paper. This can hopefully allow people to learn information from research in a way they can better understand and in a more visually appealling way.
  3. Who is the target audience?
    • The audience will be whoever wants to know information about the naked mole rat. These people can include students needing to do research for school, scientists gathering sources for research, or anyone in general who would like to know.
  4. What does the target audience want from the website?
    • The target audience would want this website to contain information about what the website is saying it has, namely information about the social sturcture of the naked mole rat. Also the audience would want to know sources that the information is based off of to prove the content isn't made up or plagarized. Another thing the target audience would want from the website is for the information to be shown in a way that is clear, cohesive, and overall more digestable than a long, boring research paper.
